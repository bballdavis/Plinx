## Promotional Text

Watch your little ones browse and play with Plex in a safe, kid‑friendly interface. Plinx makes parental controls easy and keeps media organized by age.

## Description

Plinx is a child‑safe front end for Plex that makes it simple to share videos, music and photos with your family without exposing mature content.  Designed for kids 2–10, Plinx offers:

- Clean, colourful interface with large thumbnails and easy navigation
- Built-in parental controls that respect Plex libraries and profiles
- Offline playback for car trips and long flights
- Seamless Plex integration – just sign in with your existing account

Whether you're a busy parent or a caregiver, Plinx keeps screen time fun, safe, and under your control.

> *Tip:* Use short paragraphs and bullet points to improve readability on the App Store.
